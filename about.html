About me

I am a professional trophy and medal manufacturer, with over 10 years of rich experience and expertise.

Our production line includes various materials, sizes and shapes of trophies and medals. Whether you need traditional metal trophies or innovative crystal or glass trophies, we can meet your needs. We have the ability to engrave text, patterns, and even photos on the trophy, and can customize it according to your specific requirements.

In the past ten years, we have provided countless high-quality trophies and medals for customers all over the world, including schools, companies, sports event organizers, etc. These successful cases have earned us a good reputation and credibility in the industry.

We attach great importance to each customer's unique needs and always strive to provide the highest quality products and services. Our team is composed of a group of creative professionals who can turn your ideas into reality.

If you have any needs for trophies or medals, please feel free to contact us at any time. We look forward to creating unique works for you to honor those people or things that you value.

Thank you for your attention!
